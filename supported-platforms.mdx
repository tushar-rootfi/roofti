* **Supported** indicates that the integration is readily available and can be effortlessly set up by the user without any external assistance.
* **Beta** indicates that the integration is still under development and may require specialised onboarding, such as approval or complex setup processes. Hence, we will onboard customers manually for such platforms until we ensure that the connection can be effortlessly set up by the users themselves.
